About My Homeroom

A different kind of
learning service

My Homeroom is not a tutoring app, a staffing agency, or a gig platform. We are a curated matching service — personally connecting families in Charlotte, NC with educators who are the right fit for their child, their home, and their goals.

We were founded on a simple belief: the best learning support comes from the right relationship. That means we take time to understand every family before making a recommendation — and every educator before adding them to our network.

As a My Homeroom educator you are not competing for jobs or cold-messaging strangers. We bring the right families to you — families who have specifically requested someone with your background, your approach, and your availability.

The Role

What the role looks like
day to day

Every engagement is different — that is the point. Here is what you can generally expect.

🏠

You go to the family

All sessions take place in the family's home. You are working in a calm, familiar environment where children are most comfortable. Most educators find this makes the work significantly more personal and rewarding.

📅

You set your schedule

You decide which days and hours you are available. You decide how many families you take on. Sessions are typically 60 to 90 minutes. Most educators work with two to five families per week.

🎯

You focus on one child

Every session is one-on-one. Your full attention goes to one learner — their goals, their pace, their specific needs. You are never splitting focus between multiple students.

📖

You teach your strengths

My Homeroom matches you to families based on your subject expertise and grade level experience. You are not asked to cover subjects outside your area.

💬

You communicate directly

Once matched you communicate directly with the family about scheduling, goals, and progress. My Homeroom stays available in the background if you need support.

🤝

You are supported

My Homeroom handles matching, introductions, and payment logistics. You focus on teaching. If a match is not working we address it together — you are never left alone to navigate family issues.

The Vetting Process

How we review
every educator

Every educator goes through a personal five-step review before being matched with any family. This is what makes families willing to open their homes to you.

1

Application Review

We review your background, subject expertise, grade level experience, and availability in full.

2

Identity Verification

Your identity is verified through our secure platform before approval. Non-negotiable.

3

Reference Check

One professional or family reference who can speak to your character and your work with children.

4

Founder Interview

A direct conversation with our founder. We want to know the person, not just the resume.

5

Verified Badge

Once approved you receive My Homeroom Verified status and become eligible for family matches.

Who We Are Looking For

Great educators come
from many backgrounds

You do not need a teaching license to apply. What matters most is genuine experience with children, subject knowledge, and the ability to build a real relationship with a family.

Licensed Teachers

Active or former K–12 classroom teachers looking for meaningful flexible supplemental work.

  • Current or retired NC licensed teachers
  • Teachers seeking more personal work
  • Teachers between positions
Tutors & Specialists

Experienced tutors and subject specialists with a documented track record working with students.

  • Private tutors with family references
  • Subject matter experts (STEM, languages, arts)
  • Academic coaching and test prep specialists
Graduate Students & Creatives

Graduate students in education or subject-specific fields and skilled creatives with teaching experience.

  • Education and subject-area graduate students
  • Musicians, artists, and writers who teach
  • Bilingual educators
