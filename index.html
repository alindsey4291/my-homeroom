The Process

How My Homeroom works

1

Create your free account

Sign up for free to access our curated network of vetted Charlotte educators. No credit card required — ever.

2

Browse and request your match

Filter educators by subject, grade level, and availability. Request the one who feels right for your child.

3

Select your plan

Once matched, choose the session plan that fits your family — Intro, Foundation, Growth, or Full Support.

4

Begin learning at home

Your educator comes to you. Sessions start within days of your match being confirmed.
